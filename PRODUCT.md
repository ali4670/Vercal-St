# Product

## Register

product

## Users

High-performance users, developers, and gamers who view their daily tasks as "missions." They value aesthetics as much as utility and want a tool that feels like a piece of high-tech digital hardware.

## Product Purpose

To transform a mundane task list into an immersive "Mission Control" experience. Success is defined by an interface that feels alive, tactile, and visually extraordinary—a true showcase of frontend skill.

## Brand Personality

Cyberpunk, Neon-Precision, Tactical. It is dark, high-contrast, and edgy, yet maintains the rigorous organization of a professional OS.

## Anti-references

Boring corporate SaaS, flat minimalist white-space, generic mobile app templates, "friendly" rounded corners without purpose.

## Design Principles

- **Digital Hardware**: Every UI element should feel like a physical, high-tech component with depth and weight.
- **Neon Guidance**: Use saturated cyan and red selectively to guide the eye through the hierarchy, never for decoration alone.
- **Tactile feedback**: Every interaction must feel responsive and heavy, using smooth exponential motion.
- **Data Density**: Information should be compact and structured, mimicking a heads-up display (HUD).

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA contrast for text, clear focus states for keyboard navigation, and reduced-motion alternatives for high-intensity animations.
